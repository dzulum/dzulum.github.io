                                              SOCIAL WORK
                                                    (Skit)
   I'm in skit, we are a very active group, prepare presentations for many
   children and elderly people, our goal is to give them a moment of joy,
   do puppets, skits, songs, dances, games, etc.
   We organized in groups rehearse in the evenings, and we always do
   different things, we have to write everything we do, this is very fun.
   Teachers Gerardo Castillo and Angel Jaramillo are our companions and
   they tell us what to do,
   We make presentations in schools, in the acyl school, barracks,
   quarters etc..
   It's funny because we dress up and paint their faces and know new
   people.
   To make puppets, use fabrics, wool, artificial eyes, liquid painting,
   fomix, etc.
   The theme for the dramatization should be with and fun message.
   The dance has to be folk music, using large skirts, hats, no shoes
   The song has to have message.
   Games should help children and motivate them, they receive gifts from
   us.
   In my role play group we all get along and try to make the
   presentations go well.
